About Me


'What do you wanna be when you grow up?' Every time I was asked this question as a child, I would give a different answer every time. I could wake up wanting to be a professional soccer player one morning, the next I could decide to be an architect.

Before I knew it, I was faced with the dauting task of choosing a college major. I had some previous coding experience and enjoyed it, I decided to study Computer Science. I went in with the goal of becoming a programmer after graduation, but life happens, and things change!

I suddenly found myself getting really interested in design, business, and management. I had the opportunity to get involved with and lead different initiatives and projects in college, where I got to network with a wide variety of people and hear their stories. I soon came to realize that I could merge my new found interests with my technical skills and I have not looked back since!

When I'm not in the office/classroom I enjoy getting involved with societies at Trinity, volunteering, playing the guitar, soccer, documentaries and stand-up comedy!

If you would like to hear more about my journey feel free to shoot me a message
